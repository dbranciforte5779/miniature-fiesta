Club Background

This is a newly established club, created in 2022. Matt Galla created this club after a few months of studying on his own for these exams. Galla thought that studying with a group is more productive than studying on your own for many reasons. Groups provide a good benefit when studying. If you have questions, chances are somebody in your group will know the answer. This sparked the idea of creating this club. Galla was born in Ohio and moved to North Carolina when he was 7 years old and he is currently a junior at Cox Mill High School.
